Hugh Barwell

Accordionist, entertainer, teacher & MIDI expert.

After a few years in London, I am back in York.

I am available for bookings in Yorkshire and neighbouring counties.

About me

I took up accordion in my early twenties, after playing guitar and bassoon for the previous decade, and have been ‘gigging’ ever since.

I have been booked to play in a great variety of places: restaurants, markets, fairs, vintage car rallies, an Irish ferry, a narrowboat, a Thames cruiseship, stately homes, Center Parcs, barns, public parks, city centre events, car launches, Castle Howard, hotels, care homes, conference centres.
